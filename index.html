Meet the Team

At Stage and Sound Rental, our dedicated team of professionals is the heart of our success. Each member brings a wealth of experience, expertise, and passion to ensure that every event we handle is nothing short of spectacular. Get to know the people behind the scenes who make it all happen.

Scott Franks – Founder & CEO

Scott Franks is the visionary behind Stage and Sound Rental. With over 20 years of experience in the events industry, Scott founded the company to provide top-quality AV solutions and exceptional service. His leadership and commitment to excellence have been instrumental in building a company known for its reliability and innovation.

Tabitha Grant – Event Manager

Tabitha Grant - has been with Stage and Sound Rental for over 10 years, managing events of all sizes and types. Her meticulous attention to detail and ability to anticipate client's needs ensure that every event runs smoothly. Tabitha's expertise in logistics and planning is a cornerstone of our successful event executions.

Mark Stevens – Head Technician

Mark Stevens leads our technical team with over 15 years of experience in audio-visual technology. He is responsible for overseeing the setup, operation, and maintenance of all our equipment. Mark’s problem-solving skills and technical knowledge guarantee that we deliver flawless AV experiences for our clients.

Emily Walker – Sales Executive

Emily Walker is our dynamic Sales Executive who brings a friendly and professional approach to client relations. With a background in sales and customer service, Emily is dedicated to understanding our clients’ needs and providing tailored solutions to make their events successful. Her enthusiasm and dedication help build strong and lasting client relationships.

James Anderson – Lighting Designer

James Anderson is our creative Lighting Designer with a flair for creating captivating visual experiences. With a background in theatre and concert lighting, James brings a unique artistic vision to every project. His designs enhance the ambiance of any event, making it memorable and visually stunning.

Laura Mitchell – Warehouse Manager

Laura Mitchell ensures that all our equipment is meticulously organised and ready for deployment. With her background in logistics and inventory management, Laura oversees the storage, maintenance, and transportation of our AV gear. Her efficiency and organisational skills keep our operations running smoothly.

Callum Jones – Marketing Director

Callum Jones is the creative force behind our marketing efforts. With over a decade of experience in marketing and communications, Callum develops and implements strategies to promote our services and reach new clients. His innovative ideas and keen market insights help position Stage and Sound Rental as a leader in the industry.

Our Technicians and Support Staff

Our skilled team of technicians and support staff are the backbone of Stage and Sound Rental. They work tirelessly behind the scenes to set up, operate, and dismantle equipment, ensuring that every event is executed flawlessly. Their dedication and technical expertise are vital to our success.

At Stage and Sound Rental, we pride ourselves on our team's dedication, professionalism, and passion for delivering exceptional AV solutions. Each member plays a crucial role in ensuring our clients' events are unforgettable. Thank you for getting to know us - we look forward to working with you!
